Manage Your Meeting Effectively

Plan, Invite, Attend and Report

The meeting Meeting Meter was designed based on our experience in different companies: meeting management, just like email management, has become a main issue in medium and big size companies. Too much time is spent in meetings, which are mostly inefficient.

The aim of this tool is to support employees to organize meetings when they are really necessary (need vs estimated costs and duration), to follow actions, and share Minutes of Meetings (MoM).

Plan a Meeting Easily

With shared Meeting Calendar, Odoo User's can easily find a date and time when attendee will available to join a Meeting.

Click on Date, fill the form and Odoo will automatic send invitation to attendee.

List Your Meeting Agenda

List your meeting agenda, and share it with attendee.

Shared meeting agenda should be describe out in detail so that meeting participants can do the preparation and meeting goals can be achieved effectively.

Minutes of Meeting

Users can directly create and shared minutes of meeting
